What It is

A glow-giving foundation for an instantly even skin tone


What it does

Catch the glow (and keep it) with this radiance-boosting, long-lasting foundation. The lightweight formula not only provides 10 hours of even, glowing coverage but also 10 hours of hydration in a range of shades to for every skin tone.

Suggested usage

Release the foundation onto the back of your hand. Using your brush, gently dab the product to warm it up and load it onto the brush. Apply four dots of foundation to the forehead, cheeks and chin, then blend outwards from the centre to the rest of the face, including the neck, using small circular motions.
